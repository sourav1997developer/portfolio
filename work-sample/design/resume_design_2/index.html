Your resume is a 1-page document that heightlight your acedemic backgroud, work experience, honours, and additionl details. Resume are used when applying for a jobs, internships, acedemic programmes, scientific or research positions. To put it simply, a resume is your professional image. The goal of a Resume is to showcase your experience and skills so that you may appear fit for a job.

Why should you care about writing a good resume?

  • To make a compelling case for yourself so that you get an interview call.
  • To heighlight how you can contribute, by clearly articulating skills you possess.
  • To differentiate yourself from other candidates applying for the same position.
